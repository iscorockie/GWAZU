Who We Are

MORE
THAN
A BRAND

GWAZU is a movement. Born from a collective of childhood friends in Kampala's urban suburbs, created as a way to turn creativity into value.


The name comes from gwas — Kampala slang for "homes" — a tribute to hours spent brainstorming at each other's places, fueling a passion for creativity and entrepreneurship.


Since 2017, we've grown from a group of dreamers into a full-fledged sportswear agency, delivering kits that meet national league standards.

"If you don't wear GWAZU to sweat,
you wear it to relax."
2017

Operating as a collective since 2017, officially registered in 2022. Founding members have known each other since 2005, bound by a shared vision of captivating audiences through creativity and style.
