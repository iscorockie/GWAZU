Who We Are

MORE THAN
A BRAND

GWAZU is a movement. Born from a collective of childhood friends in Kampala's urban suburbs, GWAZU was created as a way to turn creativity into value.


The name comes from gwas — Kampala slang for "homes" — a tribute to the countless hours spent at each other's places, brainstorming ideas, refining concepts, and fueling a passion for creativity and entrepreneurship.


Since 2017, we've grown from a group of dreamers into a full-fledged fashion and sportswear agency, offering locally made apparel that meets the professional standards of national leagues.

"If you don't wear GWAZU to sweat, you wear it to relax — and look good doing both."
2017

Operating as a collective since 2017, officially registered in 2022. The founding members have known each other since 2005, bound by a shared vision of captivating audiences through creativity, innovation, and style.
